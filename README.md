# StrataNode Infrastructure Ltd 

## Connecting Ireland’s Low-Altitude Economy

StratNode Infrastructure is an Irish low-altitude infrastructure originator focused on identifying, de-risking and commercialising future drone, UAS, VTOL/eVTOL and Innovative Air Mobility corridors.

This repository contains a working discussion paper intended to invite review, challenge and collaboration from aviation specialists, infrastructure partners, regulators, operators, investors and public-sector stakeholders.

## Core Model

StrataNode  identifies the corridor, de-risks the compliance pathway, commercialises the project, and works with construction joint-venture partners to deliver the physical infrastructure layer.

## The 4 Cs

- **Corridors** — identify high-value low-altitude routes and infrastructure nodes.
- **Compliance** — map the IAA, EASA, SORA, planning, insurance and privacy pathway.
- **Commercialisation** — validate demand and package viable opportunities into investable projects.
- **Construction** — delivered through qualified joint-venture or SPV construction partners.

## Discussion Paper

This repository will be developed into an open online discussion paper covering:

- the low-altitude infrastructure opportunity;
- Irish Government policy alignment;
- corridor categories;
- compliance and planning;
- commercial structures;
- construction partnerships;
- risks and failure points;
- proposed feasibility pilots.

## Status

This is an early-stage working framework for review, challenge and feedback. It is not a final investment memorandum, aviation approval document or funding offer.
